Rice industries

Rice Industries is an esteemed Indian company specializing in the production, export, and supply of rice. Established by Mr. P. Duraisamy Gounder, the company initially began with a small rice mill that, through his unwavering dedication, grew to achieve a production capacity of up to 40 tonnes per day.
